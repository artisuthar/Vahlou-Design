About Joseph Turchetti

Joe has worked in the Interior Design field for over 30 years and is well versed in Contemporary and Traditional home designs. His project experience ranges from residential to hotel interiors & corporate offices. Design work has taken him to many places – Florida, the Bahamas, Texas, Colorado, Chicago and North Carolina. After traveling extensively in Spain & Portugal Joe acquired a passion for hard surface design. He’s known for importing antique tiles and unique stone for his client projects. Joe maintains associate trade membership with ASID (American Society of Interior Designers) and his work has been published in Town and Country Magazine and aired on This Old House.
